This website centralizes Garfield club information, where you can search for clubs, find meeting times and dates, and learn more about them. You can also filter clubs by area, such as community service, as well.

This website was developed by Ahana Roy, Ben Bryant, Jessica Luu, Natalie Miller, and Simon Dao in the Projects In Computer Science class.

The Projects In Computer Science class is an advanced programming class taken after AP Computer Science. Working in teams, you will be able to create projects such as this one, making progress and having fun!
